This place was created for playing competitive Yu-Gi-Oh! and making friends

═══[ Our Cause ]═══
1) Study the Meta,
2) Playtest weekly and prepare for events,
3) Top Regionals, YCS, Nationals, Continentals, Worlds

═══[ Our Way ]═══
• We host playtesting sessions every weekend
• We review our duels in detail focusing on technical play
• We collaborate on deck building for specific events
• We analyze every deck and organize them in spreadsheets
• We keep things as simple and as functional as possible
• We update topping decklists and Meta statistics weekly

═══[ Ranking ]═══
• Based on our topping performance and contribution to the team, we
are sorted in @Slifer @Ra @Obelisk ranks

═══[ Activity ]═══
• It's mandatory to join playtesting sessions, keep notes, contribute to
decklists and spreadsheets, provide/comment replays for technical play analysis
• Being in a team outside the Academy is irrelevant. The only important
thing here is helping each other improve
• Players who don't improve or don't help others improve per format, are
removed from the Academy
• Proven exceptional team players or people who just need a break are excluded
